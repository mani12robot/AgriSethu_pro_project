Land Leasing

Turn your agricultural land into a sustainable income source

Earn Passive Income from Your Land

Own agricultural land but don't have the time, resources, or expertise to farm it? Agrisethu offers a transparent and profitable land leasing program that transforms your idle land into a steady income stream while contributing to the organic farming movement.

We handle everything—from testing and certification to farming and marketing—while you enjoy guaranteed lease payments and the satisfaction of supporting sustainable agriculture.

Why lease your land to Agrisethu

Guaranteed Income

Flexible Terms

Lease Rate Formula

Rates are reviewed annually and adjusted for market conditions

Land Size

Larger parcels often command better per-acre rates due to operational efficiency. Minimum 5 acres required.

Soil Quality

Premium rates for land with excellent soil composition, proper drainage, and high organic matter content.

Water Access

Reliable water sources increase lease value. Bonus for year-round irrigation capability.

Location

Proximity to markets, transportation infrastructure, and our operational centers affects pricing.
