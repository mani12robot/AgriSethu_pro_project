Our Story

The journey of connecting land, farmers, technology, and markets

The Birth of Agrisethu

Agrisethu was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between landowners seeking sustainable income, farmers needing quality land, and global markets demanding organic produce. Our founders recognized that the future of agriculture lies not just in traditional farming, but in the intelligent integration of technology, trust, and transparency.

What started as a small initiative to help local farmers has grown into an international organic brand, serving landowners, farmers, and markets across multiple continents. We believe that organic farming is not just a method—it's a movement toward a healthier planet and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Journey

From vision to reality - the Agrisethu timeline

2018 - The Beginning

Founded with a mission to revolutionize organic agriculture. Started with 50 acres and 5 dedicated farmers in a single region.

2019 - Technology Integration

Launched our AI-powered crop prediction system, helping farmers make data-driven decisions and optimize yields.

2020 - Organic Certification

Achieved international organic certification standards, opening doors to premium global markets.

2021 - Expansion

Expanded operations to 500+ acres across multiple regions, partnering with 150+ landowners and 200+ farmers.

2023 - Global Markets

Established direct trade relationships with organic markets in 25 countries, ensuring fair prices for farmers.

2025 - Future Forward

Continuing to innovate with advanced soil testing, sustainable practices, and blockchain-based supply chain transparency.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable organic agriculture ecosystem that benefits everyone—from landowners earning passive income, to farmers practicing their craft with dignity, to consumers receiving the healthiest produce, and to our planet healing from chemical agriculture.

We are committed to transparency, technology, and trust. Every acre we manage, every crop we grow, and every market we serve is a step toward a more sustainable future.
