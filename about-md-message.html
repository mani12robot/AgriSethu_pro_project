A Message from Our Founder

Dear Partners, Farmers, and Well-wishers,

When I founded Agrisethu, I carried with me not just a business degree but years of observing our agricultural landscape. I saw fertile lands lying unused while skilled farmers sought employment. I witnessed market demands for organic produce going unmet due to fragmented supply chains. Most importantly, I recognized the immense potential of Indian agriculture waiting to be unlocked through the right connections.

My B.Com education gave me the business framework, but my heart has always been in agriculture. This unique combination allowed me to envision Agrisethu not just as a company, but as a 'bridge' - a 'Sethu' - connecting disparate elements of our agricultural ecosystem. We bridge idle land with productive farming, traditional knowledge with modern technology, local produce with global markets, and most importantly, we bridge economic opportunity with social impact.

As the sole founder of Agrisethu, every decision, every innovation, and every partnership has been driven by a singular vision: to create a sustainable agricultural model where everyone wins. Our farmer-employee model wasn't born from business textbooks but from recognizing the dignity that stable employment brings to skilled agricultural workers. Our AI-driven crop predictions didn't come from tech trends but from understanding the uncertainty farmers face each season.

What truly excites me is witnessing Agrisethu's transformation from my vision to reality. Seeing landowners earning from previously unused land, watching farmers gain not just income but professional recognition, and observing our organic produce reaching international markets - these moments validate our unique approach.

My vision for Agrisethu's growth is indeed at the next level. We're not merely expanding operations; we're building an agricultural revolution. With plans to integrate IoT for precision farming, expand our medicinal plant cultivation, and strengthen our export capabilities, we're positioning Indian farmers as global leaders in sustainable agriculture.

Beyond business metrics, our commitment to Karnataka's communities remains unwavering. Through various social initiatives, we're working to create lasting positive impact, because true prosperity is measured not just in financial returns but in social transformation.

I invite you to join us on this transformative journey. Whether you're a landowner with untapped potential, a farmer seeking stability and dignity, or a partner who shares our vision for sustainable agriculture, Agrisethu welcomes you. Together, let's build bridges to a more prosperous and sustainable future for Indian agriculture.

With gratitude and commitment,

Chethan A
Founder & Managing Director, Agrisethu Private Limited
