Land Testing Services

Comprehensive analysis to ensure your land is perfect for organic farming

Why Land Testing Matters

Before any organic farming begins, we conduct rigorous testing to ensure your land meets the highest standards for chemical-free agriculture. Our comprehensive testing process evaluates soil health, water quality, and legal compliance to guarantee optimal conditions for organic crop production.

This scientific approach not only protects your investment but also ensures that the produce grown on your land meets international organic certification standards, commanding premium prices in global markets.

Our Testing Process

Four comprehensive steps to verify your land's organic potential

1

Soil Testing

Comprehensive analysis of soil composition, pH levels, nutrient content, and contamination history to ensure organic suitability.

2

Water Testing

Evaluation of water sources for purity, mineral content, and absence of chemical pollutants critical for organic irrigation.

3

Legal Verification

Complete documentation review including land titles, ownership verification, and compliance with agricultural regulations.

4

Organic Assessment

Final evaluation determining the land's readiness for organic certification and optimal crop recommendations.

Soil Testing Services

  • pH Level Analysis: Optimal range for organic crops
  • Nutrient Profile: NPK and micronutrient assessment
  • Organic Matter Content: Soil fertility evaluation
  • Heavy Metal Screening: Detection of toxic elements
  • Pesticide Residue Testing: Chemical contamination check
  • Microbial Activity: Soil health indicators
  • Texture Analysis: Sand, silt, and clay composition

Water Testing Services

  • Source Identification: Well, river, or irrigation system
  • Purity Testing: Bacterial and pathogen screening
  • Mineral Content: Essential and harmful minerals
  • Chemical Analysis: Pesticide and fertilizer traces
  • pH and Salinity: Irrigation suitability assessment
  • Flow Rate Evaluation: Adequate water supply verification
  • Seasonal Availability: Year-round water access

Legal Verification Process
